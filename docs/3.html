¡Aguas! Reporte Hídrico Ciudadano Android Application

Project Description

Designed and implemented an Android native application for helping a non-profit organization have record of pluvial situations in the city of Guadalajara. The data obtained by the organization Agua y Ciudad AC will be later be use to make reports to the goverment of the city and improve the infrastructure in the areas that need it most. The Application was developed by a team of three students as part of a development project for a mobile development course at Tecnológico de Monterrey.

Project Details
